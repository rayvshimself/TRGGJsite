Schedule 2018

Freitag (26.1.2018): Welcome!

Event will start in a different building (Q building) in room Q128, please review the directions. Please try to be on time for the opening presentation since we will be discussing important rules to the Global Game Jam in Trier and the theme will be revealed!
  • 14:00 Workshops
  • 16:00 Doors Open in Q128
  • 16:45 Opening Presentation
  • 17:00 Keynote
  • Reveal of the GGj Theme
  • For 30 min: Brainstorming and idea finding
  • Afterwards: Idea Pitching
  • Afterwards: Group Finding phase
  • Then: Let’s go!

    • Samstag (27.1.2018): Main Jamming!

  • Until 11:00 Last Chance to register at globalgamejam
  • 18:00 – 19:00 Playtesting of the other games

    • Sonntag (28.1.2018): Wrap it up!

  • Until 12:00 make your team page at globalgamejam.org
  • 15:00 – 16:00 Upload Phase!!!
  • 16:15 Presentation of the games
  • Afterwards: Peoples Choice Award and lottery
  • Afterwards: Good-Bye and Cleaning up everything

    • Explore the awesome games made in past GGjs at the jamsite in Trier
